Discover Digital Architecture:
Visual Guide to Scientific Knowledge


Welcome to our online platform dedicated to the elucidation of the intricacies inherent in digital architecture. Our digital space has been meticulously designed to facilitate users in the assimilation of scientific concepts inherent in the domain of digital architecture, affording particular emphasis to content emanating from CUMINCAD database.

Whether one is a seasoned professional, an aspiring student delving into the multifaceted world of architecture, or an enthusiastic explorer seeking to engage with the forefront of developments, our platform is meticulously crafted to traverse the schism between abstruse theoretical constructs and readily accessible understanding.

Through a methodical visual exploration of these scientific principles, our objective is to endow users with the capability to navigate and apprehend the nuanced intricacies of digital architecture, all within the confines of an online compendium. We cordially invite you to accompany us on this intellectual odyssey as we unlock the portals to an abundance of knowledge situated at the confluence of technology and design.

This study was conducted as part of a PhD dissertation under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Dr. Ethem GÜRER. The article derived from the PhD dissertation is available at the following link:

Karacif, E., & Gürer, E. (2025). Taxonomy and representation of digital architecture knowledge: Proposal for an online encyclopedia model. Journal of the Faculty of Engineering and Architecture of Gazi University, 40(3), 1509–1524.
